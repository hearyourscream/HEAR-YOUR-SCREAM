THE HORROR AWAITS

Welcome to a nightmare woven from the darkest threads of Japanese folklore. Hear Your Scream plunges you into a world where ancient evils have awakened, and the boundary between the living and the dead has been torn asunder.

Your journey will take you through haunted Japanese school hallways where the echoes of past tragedies still linger, desecrated shrines where corrupted spirits hunger for mortal souls, and forbidden ritual chambers where cultists perform unspeakable ceremonies in the name of forgotten gods.

The anomalies you encounter are far more than mere ghosts. This is psychological horror that will slowly erode your sanity, twisting your perception until you question what is real. Sudden jump scares will shatter your nerves when you least expect them.

But beware, amidst the terror, moments of dark comedy may catch you off guard, making you scream and laugh in the same breath. Every corner holds a new surprise, every shadow conceals a new horror.

You will face an unholy pantheon of entities: grotesque Yokai with faces stretched beyond human recognition, vengeful Oni demons wielding rusted blades, corrupted Samurai spirits bound by dishonor, and entities that wear the faces of cute girls, beautiful masks hiding unspeakable malice.

The question is not whether you will survive... but what will be left of you when it's over.
